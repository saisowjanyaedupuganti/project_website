UK Road Accident Analysis

Project Topic

This project studies reported road accidents in the United Kingdom. We use the accident dataset to look at when and where crashes happen and how different conditions affect them.

Target Audience

The website is mainly for transport planners, traffic police, policy makers and anyone interested in road safety and accident patterns.

Main Questions

  • Which locations and days of the week have more accidents?
  • How are speed limits related to accident severity?
  • How do weather, light and road surface conditions influence accidents?
  • What is the relationship between number of vehicles and number of casualties?

Dataset

The dataset includes fields such as Accident Index, Date, Day of Week, Latitude, Longitude, Weather Conditions, Light Conditions, Road Surface Conditions, Number of Vehicles and Number of Casualties.
