Exploring road accident patterns across the United Kingdom

This project uses UK accident data to understand when and where crashes happen, how severe they are, and how factors like weather, light and road surface influence safety on the road.

Interactive visual story for planners & citizens
Focus UK accidents
Audience Planners & public
Visuals 10 charts

Project goals

  • Identify hotspots and risky conditions for road accidents.
  • Compare accident severity across different speed limits.
  • Study the impact of weather, light and road surface conditions.
  • Relate number of vehicles involved to casualties.

Dataset

The dataset contains fields such as Accident Index, Date, Day of Week, Latitude, Longitude, Weather Conditions, Light Conditions, Road Surface Conditions, Number of Vehicles and Number of Casualties.
